URBAN ADVENTURERS

Ideas and inspiration to help you #FindYourFun

London Wetland Centre

Family tickets start from £33.27, children under four go free

Explore an urban oasis on the doorstep of the capital at the London Wetland Centre. Wander through the meadows and gardens and keep a keen eye on your surroundings, because who knows what wildlife you’re likely to stumble across? Home to otters, herons and many more British creatures, the Wetland Centre is the ideal destination for a wild and beautiful day out.

Find out more

Survival School

Prices vary depending on course

Inject some fun into a trip to the woods with the Survival School, Bristol. Unite as a family and learn the art of bushcraft, or sign up to a serious survival course for a day or even a weekend. Specialist instructors are on hand to ensure every participant receives an element of private tuition to equip them with the skills needed to survive in the wilderness.

Find out more

Explore Falmouth by boat

Boats available for hire from £30 an hour, open times vary throughout the year

Cornwall is synonymous with the sea, so what better way to explore it than by boat? From sail boats to motor boats, you and your family can explore the beautiful sheltered waters around Falmouth. Discover tiny villages, waterside pubs and secret places ideal for picnics. Babies and dogs are welcome aboard and full safety information and instructions are provided.

#FindYourFun
AND EXPLORE YOUR CITY WITH THE CROCS DISCOVERY TRAIL

Step 1

Download the free Geotourist app on Apple iPhone or Google Android.

Start the app and look for the crocs trails in the city closest to you. Tap to select your preferred trail and simply press “START” on the selected trail summary page to reveal the map of the location of the points. You can also use the “Route me to starting point” on the summary page to be navigated to the beginning of the trail.

Step 2

Follow the clues around your city and answer the quiz questions for each location.

Walk to each numbered point on the map and you will be able to listen out for clues about the location around – then, a quiz question will pop up for you to answer. Answer each question correctly and move on to the next location. Each time you walk into a new point, the location audio will be triggered and then the quiz question will pop up on the screen for you to answer.

Step 3

Win a Crocs medal! Complete at least one city trail to become a Crocs Champion!

Share your success with your friends on Facebook at the end of every trail!

Complete your first trail and then why not try out another Crocs #FindYourFun trail - there are 6 to choose from!
